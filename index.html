Welcome to the Infirmary

We offer a convenient way for students to book appointments with doctors, describe their health issues, and view their prescriptions online. We are dedicated to providing quality healthcare services to our students.

Our Departments

Our Health Center is equipped with specialized departments designed to cater to the diverse healthcare needs of our student community. Each department is staffed with experienced professionals dedicated to providing comprehensive and personalized care.

First Aid

Our First Aid department is dedicated to providing immediate and effective care for minor injuries and urgent medical situations.

Diagnosis

Accurate and timely diagnosis is a cornerstone of effective healthcare. At the Infermary, we offer a range of diagnostic services to help identify health issues.

Orthopedic Care

Our Orthopedic Care department is dedicated to diagnosing, treating, and managing conditions related to sports injury, chronic pain, or any other orthopedic concern,

Ambulance

We provide a reliable and efficient ambulance service in case of an urgent medical situation on campus or the need for transportation to a nearby hospital for specialized care.

About Us

Welcome to the Thapar Health Center, your dedicated partner in student health and wellness. Located conveniently on campus, our mission is to provide comprehensive healthcare services tailored to meet the needs of our diverse student community. Here, we prioritize your well-being. Whether you're seeking routine medical care, treatment for minor illnesses and injuries, or preventive health services, our team of experienced healthcare professionals is here to support you every step of the way.
